Loan Required Documents
  1. Application for loan.
  2. Aadhar card / PAN card / Voter ID (2 photocopies each).
  3. For loan amounts below 2 lakhs: any Income Proof.
  4. For loan amounts of 2 lakhs or above: ITR copy is required.
  5. CIBIL Report.
  6. Immovable property documents (e.g., Rin Pustika, Registry, Diversion in Original).
  7. SEARCH report by bank advocate.
  8. Affidavit, Agreement letter.
  9. E-Mitra papers for loans above Rs 2 lakhs.
  10. Valuation Report.
  11. Borrower's and Guarantor's photos.
  12. 20 Revenue Stamps of Rs 1 each.
  13. Borrower should deposit 5% of the loan amount, refundable after loan processing.
  14. 1% processing fee of the loan amount, not refundable.
  15. The insurance fee deposited by the borrower is not refundable.
  16. For housing loans, a passed map of the house should be approved by the municipal authority.
  17. For vehicle loans, 25% of the vehicle amount should be approved by the municipal authority.
